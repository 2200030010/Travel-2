Our Guarenteed reservation policy

In a guaranteed reservation, the hotel ensures that a room will be held for the guest, even if they arrive late in the day. Guests typically provide a form of payment or a deposit to secure the reservation. Common methods of guarantee include credit card details, prepayment, or other financial assurances.
